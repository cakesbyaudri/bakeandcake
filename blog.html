CAKES BY AUDRI

Cakes have always been a delightful passion for me, Audri Marjan Tasnim. The joy and comfort they bring, with their rich flavors and beautiful decorations, captivate my heart. Whether it's a simple vanilla sponge or an elaborate tiered confection, each cake tells a story and offers a slice of happiness that I love to share with family and friends.

My love for baking goes beyond just cakes. It's an art form that allows me to express creativity and bring smiles to those around me. The process of mixing ingredients, experimenting with flavors, and watching my creations come to life in the oven fills me with immense satisfaction. Baking is not just a hobby but a way of life that I cherish deeply.

One day, I plan to open a small bakery or cake shop to showcase my skills and share my baked goods with a wider audience. It will be a cozy place where people can enjoy delicious treats, and I can continue to pursue my passion for baking, creating memorable experiences for everyone who walks through the door.

AUDRI MARJAN TASNIM // 23229521
